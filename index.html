About Us

Welcome to AWDP & Brisbane Canine Coaching At AWDP & Brisbane Canine Coaching, we specialise in helping you and your dog build a strong, positive relationship based on trust, communication, and effective training. Whether you’re looking to correct behavioural issues, improve obedience, or raise a well-mannered companion, our team is here to support you every step of the way. With years of experience training dogs of all breeds and temperaments, we offer tailored training solutions for domestic pet owners as well as government and private business clients with working dogs. Our approach combines proven techniques with a deep understanding of canine behaviour, ensuring that both you and your dog gain the skills and confidence to thrive together. From basic obedience to advanced training and behaviour modification, our personalised services are designed to make a real difference in your dog’s life. Let us help you unlock your dog’s potential and create a harmonious and enjoyable life together. Explore our services and see how we can help you achieve the best for your dog!
