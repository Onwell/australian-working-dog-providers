Payment for Services:

To confirm your booking, a 25% deposit is required once your session has been organised and scheduled. The remaining balance will be due within seven days of receiving the final invoice.

Cancellation Policy:

If you need to cancel your session, a full refund of your 25% deposit will be provided, provided the cancellation is made with at least 48 hours’ notice before the scheduled consultation or training session.

Late Cancellation Policy:

Cancellations made within 48 hours of a scheduled consultation or training session will result in forfeiture of the 25% deposit.

For clients who have purchased a training package, the same terms and conditions apply.

If you cancel a session with more than 48 hours’ notice, the session will be rescheduled for a later date at no additional cost.

Cancellations made with less than 48 hours’ notice will incur a late cancellation fee, as outlined above.

We appreciate your understanding and cooperation.

Australian Working Dog Protection offers specialized training programs for working dogs. Our expert services ensure reliability, and you will be impressed with the results.
